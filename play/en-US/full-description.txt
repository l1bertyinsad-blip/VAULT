SAVIO keeps useful things from disappearing into endless scrolling.

Found a recipe, reel, idea, travel place or important file? Tap Share and choose SAVIO. It lands in Inbox, ready to organize now or later.

SAVIO lets you save links, photos, videos, files and notes; organize folders; add descriptions and the reason you saved something; search your own context; favorite important items; archive finished ones; and use a finite seven-card useful feed instead of infinite scrolling.

The first version is local-first, with no required account, ads or analytics trackers.

SAVIO. Save now. Find it later.
